Questions?

Please pay attention to all materials and announcements covered during class. Check your UW email account or Canvas inbox daily so that I can communicate with you between classes. If you wish to use another e-mail address as your primary account, then please set up your UW account to forward to your other address.

I will do my best to answer email on a first-come, first-served basis within 24 hours on business days. To ensure that everyone benefits from answers to questions relevant to many people, I will post the answers to some questions on Canvas.

Office hours are a great opportunity for you to get individual attention to help you meet your learning goals. I hold face-to-face office hours for the hour before class. You can also set up an appointment to meet with me by Zoom.

Dr. Price acknowledges that we live and work on the unceded, ancestral lands of indigenous people. For example, the UWB campus sits on the original territory belonging to the Willow (Sammamish) People. Let us thank them for the stewardship of this land. Let us pay our respects to elders past and present.
